Delete Your Data

If you want to delete your data, you can do so under the 'User Settings' page in your profile and under the 'Delete Your Account Permanently' setting. This will remove your account on Friendi. Note this action is permanent and irreversible, only do this if you are absolutely sure you want your account removed. Your data will be wiped in its entirety, we do not retain any records of your personal data.
